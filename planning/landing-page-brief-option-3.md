# Landing Page Brief, Option 3

## Working Offer

AI-powered growth and operations support for small businesses.

## Core Promise

We help small businesses get more customers, reduce admin, and run more smoothly by handling the digital side with smart systems and AI.

## What This Offer Is

This offer is the most business-outcome-focused of the three.

The client is not primarily buying access to an AI assistant or a custom AI system as a standalone product. They are buying help with:

- growth
- lead handling
- booking and follow-up
- reducing admin burden
- improving operations
- digital support that helps the business run better

## What This Offer Is Not

- not a generic marketing agency pitch
- not a technical product page
- not a self-serve software offer
- not centered on the underlying AI stack

## Target Visitor

Primarily non-technical small business owners and operators, especially local businesses.

Examples may include:

- solo operators
- local service businesses
- small teams that are overwhelmed by admin
- business owners who want more customers without managing the digital complexity themselves

## Visitor Mindset

"I want more customers and less admin. I do not want to deal with websites, follow-up, booking, and digital systems myself."

## Primary Goal of the Page

Capture interest from small businesses that want help improving growth and operations without having to become technical.

## Positioning

This page should be the least technical of the three.

The emphasis should be on:

- more leads
- better follow-up
- less admin
- smoother day-to-day operations
- more time spent doing the real business work

## Messaging Priorities

1. Focus on running your business
2. We help handle the digital side
3. Reduce missed leads and repetitive admin
4. Use smart systems and AI behind the scenes
5. Make the business easier to run and easier to grow

## Key Differentiator

This offer is business-result-first.

Unlike Option 1 and Option 2, the page should not feel like a technology service first. It should feel like a business support and growth offer powered by smart systems.

## Headline Directions

- More customers, less admin
- We help small businesses run and grow more efficiently
- We handle the digital side so you can run the business
- Get more leads and spend less time on admin
- Smart systems for small businesses that want to grow

## Subheadline Directions

- From lead follow-up to booking and digital operations, we help small businesses save time and capture more opportunities.
- We handle the digital systems behind the scenes so you can focus on the work your business actually does.
- We help small businesses reduce admin, improve follow-up, and support growth with practical systems and AI.

## CTA Directions

Primary CTA options:

- Contact Us
- See How We Can Help
- Ask About Growth Support
- Talk to Us

Recommended primary CTA for testing:

- See How We Can Help

## Key Benefits

- less time spent on repetitive admin
- fewer missed leads and follow-ups
- better support for bookings and customer communication
- more time focused on the actual business
- practical help without needing technical knowledge

## Example Use Cases

- lead follow-up support
- booking and scheduling support
- inbox and response handling
- website and contact flow support
- operational reminders and coordination
- reducing digital busywork for owners and staff

## Recommended Page Sections

1. Hero
2. What we help with
3. Problems we solve
4. Why small businesses use this kind of support
5. How it works
6. Contact form
7. FAQ

## Recommended Tone

- approachable
- trustworthy
- business-first
- calm and simple
- least technical of the three

## Visual Direction

- light background, soft white or cream
- dark text with a warmer accent palette
- subtle blue-green or muted warm accent colors
- friendly but professional layout
- no heavy technical visuals

## Trust Strategy

This page needs especially strong clarity and simplicity.

Trust should come from:

- plain language
- immediate business relevance
- easy contact path
- confident, non-flashy design
- examples that sound familiar to small business owners

## FAQ Topics

- What kinds of businesses is this for?
- Do I need to be technical?
- What kinds of tasks can this help with?
- Will I have to manage the systems myself?
- What happens after I contact you?
- Is this only for local businesses?

## Contact Form Strategy

Recommended fields:

- name
- email
- business name
- optional message

## Follow-Up Logic

After submission, email to gather:

- what kind of business they run
- where they are losing time or customers
- what parts of operations feel messy or manual
- what kind of support would help most
- how urgent the need feels

## What Success Looks Like

The visitor should quickly understand:

- this is for small businesses that want help with growth and operations
- they do not need to be technical
- the team helps reduce digital burden and improve business flow
