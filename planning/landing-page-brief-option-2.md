# Landing Page Brief, Option 2

## Working Offer

Done-for-you AI systems built and maintained for businesses.

## Core Promise

You tell us what you need, and we build, host, and maintain the AI workflow or system for you.

## What This Offer Is

This offer is for businesses that want a solution, not access to the underlying assistant environment.

The client is buying:

- a custom AI-enabled solution
- delivery around a specific workflow or problem
- hosting and maintenance
- ongoing operation of the built system

## What This Offer Is Not

- not direct access to a general AI assistant platform
- not a generic business growth service
- not just consulting advice
- not a DIY software product

## Target Visitor

Businesses that:

- have a specific process they want improved
- want automation or AI help without learning the system themselves
- care more about the result than the tooling
- want a service partner to build and maintain the solution

## Visitor Mindset

"I do not want another tool to manage. I want someone to build the thing I need and keep it working."

## Primary Goal of the Page

Capture interest from businesses that want a done-for-you AI solution tailored to a real workflow problem.

## Positioning

This is a custom implementation service.

The client should feel:

- they do not need to touch the underlying AI system
- they can describe the business problem
- the team will handle the build and maintenance

## Messaging Priorities

1. Tell us the problem you want solved
2. We build the solution for you
3. We host and maintain it
4. You receive the result, not the technical burden
5. This can apply across many business workflows

## Key Differentiator

Unlike Option 1, the client is not getting direct assistant access as the core offer.

They are getting a built outcome.

## Headline Directions

- We build AI systems for your business
- Done-for-you AI workflows for real business tasks
- Tell us what you need, we build the AI system for it
- Custom AI systems, built and maintained for you
- AI solutions for your business, without the technical overhead

## Subheadline Directions

- From email workflows to scheduling and internal operations, we build and maintain AI systems around the work your business actually needs done.
- We design, build, host, and maintain AI-powered workflows so you do not have to manage the technical side.
- If you know the problem you want solved, we can build the system around it.

## CTA Directions

Primary CTA options:

- Contact Us
- Tell Us What You Need
- Request a Custom Build
- Ask About a Custom System

Recommended primary CTA for testing:

- Tell Us What You Need

This improves fit without adding much friction.

## Key Benefits

- no need to learn or manage the system yourself
- tailored to your workflow
- ongoing hosting and maintenance included
- flexible across many business tasks
- outcome-focused instead of tool-focused

## Example Use Cases

Examples should be concrete and practical.

- AI email assistant for inbox handling
- scheduling and calendar automation
- lead intake or triage workflows
- recurring reports or analytics support
- internal process automation
- customer follow-up workflows

## Recommended Page Sections

1. Hero
2. What we build
3. Example use cases
4. Why businesses choose done-for-you systems
5. How it works
6. Contact form
7. FAQ

## Recommended Tone

- practical
- service-oriented
- clear
- confident
- more implementation-focused than Option 1

## Visual Direction

- warm white or soft neutral background
- dark slate text
- muted teal or green accent
- clean cards for use case examples
- structured layout that feels process-driven

## Trust Strategy

Since proof may be limited early on, trust should come from:

- specific examples
- transparent explanation of the service
- a clear process
- clean professional design
- straightforward copy

## FAQ Topics

- What kinds of systems can you build?
- Do I need to use the software directly?
- Is this customized to my business?
- Do you host and maintain it?
- What happens after I reach out?
- Can this work for non-technical teams?

## Contact Form Strategy

Recommended fields:

- name
- email
- business name
- optional message

Optional alternate field if desired later:

- what do you want help with?

## Follow-Up Logic

After contact, reply by email to gather:

- what their business does
- what process they want improved
- how they currently handle it
- why it matters
- what result they want

## What Success Looks Like

The visitor should quickly understand:

- they can describe a business problem
- the team can build a system around it
- they do not need to manage the underlying technical complexity
