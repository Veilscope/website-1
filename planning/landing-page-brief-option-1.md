# Landing Page Brief, Option 1

## Working Offer

Managed AI assistant access for small businesses.

## Core Promise

We set up, host, secure, and maintain a business AI assistant for you. You get access to use it, while we handle the technical side.

## What This Offer Is

This offer gives the client direct access to a hosted AI assistant environment.

The client is not buying a custom-built one-off automation deliverable. They are buying:

- setup
- hosted access
- maintenance
- security oversight
- a working AI assistant they can use however they want

## What This Offer Is Not

- not a generic marketing service
- not a done-for-you custom automation build
- not a full business growth service
- not a training-heavy technical product page

## Target Visitor

Small business owners, operators, or teams who:

- want access to AI capability
- do not want to install or manage it themselves
- want something ready to use
- may be curious about how AI can help their business day to day

## Visitor Mindset

"I want an AI assistant for my business, but I do not want to deal with setup, hosting, maintenance, or security."

## Primary Goal of the Page

Capture a contact submission from visitors interested in having a managed AI assistant installed and hosted for their business.

## Positioning

Outcome-first, not tool-first.

The page should emphasize the result:

- a ready-to-use business AI assistant
- managed setup and hosting
- no technical overhead on the client side

OpenClaw should remain backstage and should not be a primary marketing term.

## Messaging Priorities

1. We handle the technical setup
2. You get direct access to a working AI assistant
3. We host and maintain it for you
4. You can use it across many business tasks
5. It is simple to get started

## Key Differentiator

The client gets direct access to the assistant itself.

This is the main difference from Option 2, where the client gets a finished solution instead of direct access to the underlying assistant environment.

## Headline Directions

- Get a hosted AI assistant for your business
- Your business AI assistant, set up and managed for you
- We install and host your business AI assistant
- Get business AI access without the technical setup
- A ready-to-use AI assistant for your business

## Subheadline Directions

- We set up, host, secure, and maintain your AI assistant so you can start using it without handling the technical side.
- Get a business AI assistant that is installed, hosted, and maintained for you.
- We handle the infrastructure and maintenance so your team can focus on using the assistant.

## CTA Directions

Primary CTA options:

- Contact Us
- Ask About Setup
- Get Started
- Ask About Hosted Access

Recommended primary CTA for testing:

- Contact Us

Reason: clean, low-friction, easy to reuse in demand testing.

## Key Benefits

- No installation or infrastructure work on your side
- Hosted and maintained for you
- Direct access to your AI assistant
- Flexible for many business use cases
- Less technical friction to getting started

## Example Use Cases

Use cases should remain broad but concrete.

Examples:

- drafting emails and responses
- answering internal questions
- organizing business knowledge
- helping with repetitive admin tasks
- assisting with research and planning
- supporting day-to-day operations

## Recommended Page Sections

1. Hero
2. What this is
3. What you can use it for
4. Why businesses choose managed access
5. How it works
6. Contact form
7. FAQ

## Recommended Tone

- modern
- clean
- capable
- professional
- slightly technical, but still plain-language

## Visual Direction

- off-white background
- charcoal or dark slate text
- muted blue accent
- clean spacing
- minimal graphics
- subtle interface-inspired visual treatment if needed

Avoid loud "AI startup" visuals.

## Trust Strategy

Because this is an early validation page, trust should come from:

- clear explanation
- restrained design
- confident language
- simple process
- low-friction contact

## FAQ Topics

- What kind of businesses is this for?
- What can I use the AI assistant for?
- Do I need to set anything up myself?
- Do you host and maintain it?
- Is this meant for non-technical businesses too?
- What happens after I contact you?

## Contact Form Strategy

Keep it light.

Recommended fields:

- name
- email
- business name
- optional message

## Follow-Up Logic

After submission, reply by email with qualifying questions about:

- business type
- intended use cases
- team size or context if relevant
- level of interest

## What Success Looks Like

The visitor should understand within seconds:

- this is a hosted AI assistant for business use
- the technical work is handled for them
- they can contact the team to learn more
